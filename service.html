IT ASSET SERVICES & MANAGEMENT

IT Asset Management ITAM and IT Service Management ITSM is a methodology employed by almost all businesses in different formats. It helps the organization to derive different values, optimize operations and resources and reduce operational costs. Some premier research establishments reported 40 to 45% operational cost savings with the deployment of ITAM and ITSM.

There are tremendous challenges IT and Help Desk managers face while managing IT Assets. The processes of procurement, inventory management, assigning, problem resolution, change, physical verification, and disposal are full of challenges that inhibit the implementation of smooth and seamless IT Operations. Synthify has developed a complete ecosystem for ITAM and ITSM which gives your IT team access to your organization assets life-cycle data from the moment you request the asset through the time it expires or it is decommissioned. It makes your IT infrastructure transparent while offering insight into the system and equipment, location of components, uses and cost of assets, asset warranty and expiration etc.

AMS CORE is an assets management system that has different sub-modules that support the organization's ASSETS complete life cycle from ASSETS ordering to ASSETS retirement. The AMS core has modules like Procurement which further has a new procurement tab by which any user of the system requests for procurement. The nine hierarchical approval cycle is configurable in nature and can be configured as per the organizational processes.

Post the approval cycle is completed the RFQ is generated automatically. Once the RFQ is responded the respondents are listed as per low to high quotes. Once the final approver approves by checking a tick box, the PO is automatically generated and sent to the vendor. This helps to reduce the time of the purchase cycle and thereby the IT managers. The system has provisions for PO recall and rejection as well. The second tab is List Procurement which lists down all procurements and the stages they are in. SYNC FTP is an option to receive the responded RFQs. In addition, we also have a tender management module that publishes and manages the tendering processes hiding the vendor's details from the approvers to avoid any partiality. It follows the law of the land where it is deployed like CAG's recommendation in India for the requirement of reverse bidding
